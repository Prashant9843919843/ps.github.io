R E A S O N

We both had reasons. I decided to stay while you decided to run away. You were fed up while I was wondering we should be together more. How can I be reasonable? To me our love was everything and you were my whole life. It is not very pleasant to realize that to you it was only an episode. And I always feel sorry for you. I'm always sorry because I hurt you. You wanted to leave but I always followed you. It's something I must live with every day. And all the pain I put you through, I wish that I could take it all away and be the one who catches all your tears. Be the one who loves you cares you and protect you. But you’re already fed up with me. You had the reasons to stay away from me, to live a new life happily. I had the reason to stay away from you, to make you happy and live my own life as if it’s a curse. The sad part is, that I will probably end up loving you without you for much longer than I loved you when I knew you. Some people might find that strange. But the truth of it is that the amount of love you feel for someone and the impact they have on you as a person is in no way relative to the amount of time you have known them.
