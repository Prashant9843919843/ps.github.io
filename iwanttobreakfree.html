I want to break free

These days, I feel like I’m being controlled by someone else. I mean I have lost control over myself That’s not who I am that’s somebody else. I used to have fixed routines My own way of life, my own world. But these days it seems like a new world A world where only I’m dim others are bold. Why am I unable to figure out? What’s the reason? Why I’m lost? I feel like I’m trapped inside a cupboard Can’t breathe & my lungs are about to burst. I feel suffocated to be myself I’m tired of being who I am now I want a change, I want to be myself And I want to break free no matter how.
