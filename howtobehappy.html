How to be Happy

Life is hard.
You try your everything to make your near ones happy but somewhereyou forget yourself. You try to take care of the people around you, you try to please them and make them happy. In that rush or let's say tension, you totally forget what you want and what makes you happy.
Be honest and ask yourself a question, when was the last time you tried to do something for yourself? When was the lad time you spent with yourself figuring out your strength and weaknesses, your likes and dislikes.
People often says, human beings are selfish, ask yourself are you selfish? If you don't remember when was the last time you actually took care of yourself then congratulations you aren't selfish. But if you rmemebr then sorry you're selfish.
In in this case I have s=no shame in saying that I am selfish.
why?
Because if I don't take care of myself the who else will? If I don't know myself then who else will recognize me? For me to be succesful one day Ii need to know my strengths and weaknesses.
Now let's move to the begining. You're tired of pleasing your closed ones but it isn't happening. try taking care of yourself first. If you are happy then only you can make people around you happy. And for that you have to take care of yourself.
Have you ever seen a standup comedian sad or crying in the stage, not in the act I mean literally? Answer is no. Comedians always come with positive aura and happy vibes even though their jokes aren't funny enough they try to make us laugh whislist they themself being happy. So, firstof all please yourself, make yourself happy, then only think about others.
